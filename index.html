Features

Everything your run needs

From solo training to crew runs, RunPose gives you the tools to improve every stride.

🦾

Real-Time Pose Detection

AI tracks 30+ body keypoints every frame — cadence, stride length, arm swing, foot strike — as you run.

📊

Form Analysis

Get instant feedback on posture, lean angle, knee lift, and ground contact time. Know what to fix before injury strikes.

🎙️

AI Coaching

Voice-guided coaching adapts to your form in real time. Like having a personal coach running beside you.

⏱️

Pace Making

Set your target pace, and RunPose keeps you there — with alerts when you drift too fast or too slow.

👥

Crew Sync

Connect your running crew. See everyone's pace, form score, and position on a live map — run together, even apart.

📈

Progress Tracking

Session-by-session history shows how your form and speed evolve. Celebrate the gains, target the gaps.

Up and running in seconds

1

2

Review & improve
